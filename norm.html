Norm: A Sustainable Marketplace

Creating a Marketplace for the Environmentally Conscious Customer

  • Role: UX Researcher, UI Designer
  • Tools used: Figma, Lucidchart, Optimalsort, Notion
  • Duration: Dec 2021 - Jan 2022, 7 weeks (20 - 40 hours a week)

    Sustainable living is more than just a trend—it's a mindset. Once you begin integrating sustainable practices into your daily routine, it becomes a natural part of everything you do. However, for environmentally conscious customers, researching ethical brands and determining which purchases have a positive impact can be time-consuming. That's why I created Norm—a sustainable marketplace where customers can easily shop for eco-friendly goods!

    This project aims to help customers have a one-stop shop for everyday purchases from brands that are rigorously vetted to meet our sustainability standards. The website is meant to be easy to use and should promote sustainable practices to help our customers live a more eco-conscious life. This project is based on a fictional brand.

External Research and User Research Goals

  • Identify opportunities and trends in the marketplace today
  • Identify competitors and their strengths and weaknesses
  • Identify best practices for organizing information on e-commerce websites
  • Identify target demographic and customer base
  • Identify customer pain points with existing websites
  • Identify current industry gaps
