Rethinking masterclass

Enhancing the MasterClass App: New Features and Improvements

  • Role: UX Researcher, UI Designer
  • Tools used: Figma, Lucidchart, Optimalsort, Notion
  • Duration: Jan 2021 - Feb 2022, 4 weeks (20 hours a week)

    MasterClass is an educational streaming platform where users can learn from renowned celebrities and experts. With over 1.5 million subscribers, the platform has gained significant popularity. The goal of redesigning the mobile app is to address current user pain points and introduce new features to enhance its usability and appeal.

    Identify pain points in the current MasterClass app and develop solutions to enhance the user experience. The redesigned app should be both visually appealing and user-friendly, incorporating new features that streamline the e-learning process.
